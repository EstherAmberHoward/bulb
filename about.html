About

Swedish multimedia illustrator with a Montessori background. When conceptualising her work, Esther uses 2D illustration as a reference for her physical pieces and delves into the realm of digital illustration, featuring stylish textures and similar whimsical themes to her textiles focused work. She enjoys the synergistic process of working collaboratively and is interested in working with artists with similar interests and depth to their practice. Her work centres on texture and tactility, and this can even be seen in her digital work, as she tends to always use at least one aspect of traditional mark making within polished digital pieces. Loves exploring printmaking, and has particularly fallen in love with the process of risograph.
